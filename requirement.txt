Step 1: Enable Developer Options on Android
Open your Android phone settings.

Scroll to About Phone.

Tap Build Number 7 times quickly.

You’ll see a message: “You are now a developer!”

Step 2: Enable USB Debugging
Go back to Settings.

Open Developer Options (usually under System → Advanced).

Enable USB Debugging.

Confirm any security prompts.
